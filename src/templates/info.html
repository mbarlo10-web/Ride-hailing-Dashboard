Welcome to Sky Harbor Airport

This page provides information about ride-hailing services at Sky Harbor Airport.

Understanding the Dashboard

Parking Zone Status

The left side of the dashboard shows parking zones with color-coded status:

Green - Available (0 rides)
Yellow - Moderate (1-2 rides)
Red - Busy (3+ rides)

Choose a green or yellow zone for shorter wait times.

Active Rides Queue

The right side shows active rides with:

  • License plate numbers
  • Service type (Uber, Lyft, etc.)
  • Estimated wait time
  • Request timestamp

How to Use

  1. Order your ride through your preferred app (Uber, Lyft, etc.)
  2. Check the dashboard to see which zones are less crowded
  3. Go to a green or yellow zone for faster pickup
  4. Find your ride by matching the license plate on the dashboard
  5. Wait in your assigned zone until your driver arrives

Tips

  • Zones update every 5 seconds - check regularly for best availability
  • License plates are displayed in real-time - watch for your ride
  • Wait times are estimates - actual times may vary
  • Have your phone ready to contact your driver if needed
